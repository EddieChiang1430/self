About ChillTech

A trusted partner for industrial cooling solutions with decades of experience and a commitment to excellence.

Our Story

ChillTech is a leading provider of industrial cooling and temperature control solutions. With over a decade of experience and production bases in Shenzhen, Suzhou, and Tianjin, we deliver high-performance chillers, modular units, laboratory systems, heat pumps, thermal oil heaters, cooling towers, and custom solutions for industries worldwide.

Serving sectors from aerospace and automotive to pharmaceuticals, electronics, and research labs, our products combine reliability, energy efficiency, and precision. Built with premium European, American, and Japanese components and certified to ISO 9001:2015, CE, CCC, and IEC standards, ChillTech ensures quality you can trust.

Driven by Innovation, Quality, Integrity, Focus, and Service, we partner with clients globally to create efficient, sustainable, and tailored temperature control solutions.
