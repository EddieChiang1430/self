Air-Cooled Screw Chillers

High-capacity air-cooled systems with screw compressors for industrial applications requiring reliable and efficient cooling.

Key Features

  • The unit includes inverse phase, high and low pressure, thermal overload, low level, antifreeze protection, etc
  • The unit use high strength welded frame
  • Imported brand compressor from Europe and Japan
  • Water tank and water pipe made of stainless steel SUS304
  • Refrigeration valve from Danfoss, Emerson, Sporlan and other well-known brands
  • Electrical components supplied from ABB, SIEMENS, Schneider and other famous brands
  • The standard unit has remote switch interface and fault transmission interface, Rs485 optional
  • Adopts microcomputer automatic control or PLC control
  • Other refrigerants can be selected (R22, R407C, R404A, R134A, R410A)
  • Other evaporator type optional (stainless steel plate heat exchanger, shell and tube, coil type, acid & alkali resistant)
  • Default color RAL 7000, metal color RAL 9005, customizable
